Section 02

About This Website & Our Programs

This Website is operated by Credit Union Pension & Benefits Trust, an umbrella entity comprising two core programs serving credit union employees and their families across British Columbia:

BC Credit Union Employees' Pension Plan

A multi-employer registered pension plan registered under the Pension Benefits Standards Act (BC) and the Pension Benefits Standards Act, 1985 (Canada). The Plan is governed by a Board of Trustees and offers three divisions:

  • 1.75% Contributory Defined Benefit Division — a guaranteed lifetime pension based on a final average earnings formula
  • 1.20% Non-Contributory Defined Benefit Division — a formula-based lifetime pension with all-service Normal Retirement Age of 65
  • Money Purchase Division — a defined contribution arrangement established in 1971, where retirement income depends on contributions and investment returns

BC Credit Union Employee Benefits Trust

A multi-employer employee life and health trust continued under the Income Tax Act (Canada) effective January 1, 2021. The Benefits Trust acts as a buyer group, delivering economies of scale to participating credit unions. Available benefits include:

  • Life Insurance and Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D)
  • Short-term and Long-term Disability insurance
  • Extended Health Care (prescription drugs, paramedical, out-of-country emergency)
  • Dental and Vision Care
  • Employee & Family Assistance Program (EFAP+) and mental health support
  • Virtual Wellness and Teladoc Health services
  • Family Building Benefits
  • Health Care Spending Accounts (HCSA)
  • Perkopolis employee perks
